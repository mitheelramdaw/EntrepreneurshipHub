HACKATHONS & HUDDLES:

The MakerSpace at Cheat Code Inc


"support entrepreneurs with the technical expert advice and support, digital branding, web development, access to 3D printing, CAD and CAM software as well as prototype development and testing through partner labs. Our scope of work focuses strictly on high-impact startups, which have built, or are building, disruptive and dynamic tech-based products and/or services for various industry sectors. These include agriculture, education, energy, financial services, health, mobile gaming, mobility, services, tourism and logistics."

ANGEL FUNDING AND VENTURE CAPITAL:


"Cheat Code Inc through its partners and network globally provides Angel Funding and Venture Capital for promising young companies in our key priority sectors. We also support startups to access various markets and networks in their industry."

ACCELERATOR PROGRAMME:


This programme is designed for startups that are post-revenue and ready to scale, access to markets and attract investments into their company. The Accelerator programme will help startups understand the valuation of companies, scale, investment readiness framework, options for growth investment restructuring, understanding term sheets, and financial models."

INCUBATION PROGRAMME:


"The Cheat Code Inc Incubation programme is designed for entrepreneurs who are in the process of starting or launching their new venture. The programme focuses on the identified market opportunity, scrumming and helping you launch your startup; growth strategy; and how best to finance and ensure that your startup is profitable."

JUNIORS Youth Weekend Programme:


"GEN Juniors is Cheat Code Inc’s Entrepreneurship Club targeted at participants between the ages 14 and 18. The programme is designed to inspire a culture of entrepreneurship and stimulate innovative idea generation amongst young people through a series of interactive Business Model Development workshops."
